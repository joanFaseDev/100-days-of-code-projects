Shigeru Miyamoto

One of the most influential figure in the gaming industry

Here are fifteen's notable accomplishments throughout Miyamoto's career:

  • 1981Designed the game "Donkey Kong," introducing the characters Mario (originally known as Jumpman) and Donkey Kong.
  • 1985Directed and produced "Super Mario Bros.," a critically acclaimed game that established many conventions of the platforming genre.
  • 1986Created "The Legend of Zelda," an action-adventure game that became a flagship franchise for Nintendo.
  • 1990Produced "Super Mario Bros. 3," which expanded on the success of the previous Mario games and introduced new power-ups and gameplay elements.
  • 1996Directed and produced "Super Mario 64," a groundbreaking 3D platformer that set a new standard for the genre.
  • 1998Produced "The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time," widely regarded as one of the greatest video games of all time.
  • 2001Produced "Super Smash Bros. Melee," a highly popular crossover fighting game featuring Nintendo characters.
  • 2002Directed and produced "Super Mario Sunshine," introducing a water-based gameplay mechanic and a new setting.
  • 2006Produced "The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess," a critically acclaimed entry in the Zelda series.
  • 2007Produced "Super Mario Galaxy," a platformer set in space, praised for its innovative level design and gameplay mechanics.
  • 2011Produced "The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword," featuring motion controls and an origin story for the series.
  • 2013Produced "Super Mario 3D World," a multiplayer platformer that received widespread acclaim.
  • 2015Created the concept and characters for "Splatoon," a colorful and unique multiplayer shooter.
  • 2017Produced "Super Mario Odyssey," a highly acclaimed 3D platformer featuring a sandbox-style gameplay approach.
  • 2020Worked on the production and design of "Super Nintendo World," a theme park dedicated to Nintendo's franchises.

You can learn more about the reasons why Shigeru Miyamoto is among the most influential designers in the history of video games on his Wikipedia entry.
